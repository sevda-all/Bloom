CARE TIPS

Fresh flowers from Coral Bloom are delivered in their original packaging, which must be removed when installed in a vase. Do not cut the binding the bouquet to preserve shape.

However you do it, cut 2-3 cm from each stem. Repeat it every 2-3 days.



Arrange in a vase and enjoy. We make every effort to include long lasting varieties of flowers which ensures that your gift will stay fresh for as long as possible.
